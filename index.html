AquaSentry

Ensuring Safe Water, Empowering Rural India

The Problem

In many rural areas, contaminated handpump and tap water cause illness and waste resources.

The Idea

A smart, solar-powered tap add-on that tests water quality in real time and shows results on a small display.

How It Works

Inside the tap, sensors measure pH, turbidity, and temperature. Data is processed and displayed instantly.

Power & Sustainability

A compact solar panel powers the system, ensuring long-term, maintenance-free operation even in remote areas.

Future Vision

Data logs stored hourly help track water safety trends and alert communities about contamination early.
